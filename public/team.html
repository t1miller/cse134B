About Us

OverDex was created by two students at UC San Diego, Sherman Cheung and Trent Miller. Their enthusiasm for Overwatch led them to create OverDex to enable competitive Overwatch players to track their stats. They are each depicted below by their favorite character respectively.

Sherman Cheung

Sherman is a fourth year Computer Engineering student at UC San Diego. His favorite Hero is Torbjorn.

Trent Miller

Trent is a fourth year Computer Engineering student at UC San Diego. His favorite Hero is Hanzo. He is an aspiring Gold rank player currently grinding it out it Bronze.
